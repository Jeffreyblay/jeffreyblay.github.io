### Text Data Mining with R 

**Objective:** To analyze research on floodwater depth estimation to identify key methods, metrics, and relationships between flood types and data variables.

**Method:** Applied R text analytics libraries (wordcloud2, tidyverse, tidygraph, FactoMineR, dplyr, ggplot2) to extract, process, and visualize text data from research papers.

**Outcome:** Highlighted the main evaluation metrics and visualized how different flood types and data variables interact, providing insights useful for environmental modeling and flood-risk assessment.

### Multivariate Data Analysis with R - Environmental Index Prediction

**Objective:** To analyze global environmental survey data (31,000 responses from 38 countries) to identify key predictors of environmental index using General Linear Models (GLM) and two-way ANOVA, providing insights applicable to policy, sustainability, and environmental strategy.

**Method:** Applied R for multivariate analysis and visualization using the car library to process survey data, perform GLM modeling, and generate interaction and residual plots for model assessment.

**Outcome:** Highlighted the most impactful indicators, Education, Age, Gender, and Country, for predicting environmental index, providing actionable insights for environmental planning and policy assessment.
