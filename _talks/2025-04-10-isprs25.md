**Presentation Topic: Advanced Geo-Data Analytics and AI for 3D Flood Mapping to Protect Built Assets.**

Our research on combining advanced geospatial data analytics with artificial intelligence to generate accurate 3D flood maps, was presented at the 2025 ISPRS Geospatial Week. The work demonstrates how these approaches can provide actionable insights for protecting built infrastructure and enhancing urban resilience. Delivered to an international audience, the presentation highlights the integration of AI and big geo-data in modern flood risk management and disaster preparedness strategies.
